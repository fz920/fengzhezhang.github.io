As a Research Assistant at the University of Cambridge, I am designing diffusion-based generative samplers for molecular energy functions.

The current direction focuses on improving sampling speed and reliability for scientific targets where evaluation cost matters. Early experiments halve sampling time versus a baseline DDPM setup.
